Extra instructions

  • For the best experience, an internet connection is required to download the font needed for the frame to appear as intended. This however, works fine with the system monospace font, but is less pretty.
  • To view the video again once you're done or just reset you can simply refresh this page. That wouldve been another 3 lines of JS im too lazy to write.
  • If the thing shows the "renderer has not started" message, try checking if your browser supports h.264, your computer is powerful enough to run this, and that you have enough RAM.
  • Maybe, you might not belive this is real text. In that case, select the frame, copy the text on it, and paste it into your text editor. I genuinely hate when people doubt these kinds of things.
